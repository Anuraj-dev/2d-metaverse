# Hyprverse Product Context

Hyprverse is a persistent online social environment for students. Its language distinguishes the social destination itself from the activities that happen inside it.

## Language

**Student Social World**:
A persistent digital third place where students form recurring relationships and spend time together through conversation, play, events, collaboration, and optional study.
_Avoid_: Study replacement, game portal, virtual LMS

**Launch Community**:
The first bounded student group invited into Hyprverse, selected because its members share an identity and can be present together often enough to make the social world feel alive.
_Avoid_: General public, all students, anonymous audience

**Social Momentum Loop**:
The recurring experience in which a student enters the world, notices familiar people, joins them, chooses a shared activity, earns or changes something together, and naturally continues into another activity.
_Avoid_: Screen-time loop, isolated mini-game loop, study-only loop

**Cooperative Campus Mission**:
A short, rotating objective completed with other students across the social world's existing activities, producing a shared outcome that encourages the group to continue together.
_Avoid_: Solo grind quest, disconnected daily task, mandatory study assignment

**Shared Progression**:
Persistent progress earned primarily through useful group participation and expressed through collective unlocks or visible changes to the social world.
_Avoid_: Raw-hours leaderboard, individual power grind, pay-to-win progression

**Personal Recognition**:
Non-competitive evidence of an individual's contributions and identity, expressed through cosmetics, titles, collections, or contribution history without granting stronger abilities.
_Avoid_: Power level, productivity score, time-spent rank
